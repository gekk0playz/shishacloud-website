Our Story

Where Every Cloud
Tells a Story

Born from a passion for authentic flavours and warm hospitality, Shisha Cloud is Almada's premier lounge experience. We blend traditional shisha culture with a contemporary, relaxed atmosphere — a place where time slows down.

Whether you're settling in for a long night with friends or unwinding solo, our curated selection of premium tobacco blends and handcrafted cocktails ensures every visit is uniquely yours.
